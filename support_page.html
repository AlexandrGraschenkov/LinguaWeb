How to cancel a subscription

Open the App Store on your iPhone, tap your profile icon, then choose Subscriptions. Find Lingüa Player in the list of active subscriptions and select the cancellation option.

How to request a refund

If you were charged by mistake, the refund request must be submitted to Apple. Sign in on Apple's website, choose the purchase, and request a refund there.

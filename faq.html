Questions people usually ask before using Lingüa Player.

Short answers about video sources, offline playback, translators, flashcards, and privacy.

Can I watch YouTube and other online videos?

Yes. Lingüa Player is designed for online video sources, including YouTube. Availability can depend on the source website, network access, and platform restrictions.

Can I add offline movies and TV shows?

Yes. You can add local video files and use Lingüa Player as a flexible offline player for formats that are often not supported by the default iOS player, including MKV-style workflows.

How does subtitle translation work?

When subtitles are available, you can tap words or phrases, translate them, and save useful vocabulary as flashcards.

Can the app translate text visible inside a video frame?

Yes. When you pause the video, Lingüa Player can recognize visible text in the frame and translate it into your native language.

Does Lingüa Player work offline?

Yes. If your video and subtitles are available offline, you can study without internet. The app also includes an offline translation option for private, connection-free learning.

Which translators are supported?

Lingüa Player supports multiple translation providers, including online translators and a fully offline translator. The exact set may vary by app version and selected language pair.

How do flashcards work?

Saved words can be reviewed as flashcards with spaced repetition, similar in spirit to Anki-style review. More exercise types are planned for future versions.

What data does the app collect?

The app may collect usage analytics, crash diagnostics, device/app information, and purchase-related data needed to operate and improve the app. See the Privacy Policy for details.

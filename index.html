Learn languages with the videos you already want to watch.

Lingüa Player turns online video, YouTube, downloaded movies, TV shows, and unusual video formats into a language-learning workspace with subtitle translation, OCR, flashcards, and offline translation.

From watching to remembering, without leaving the video.

Lingüa Player keeps the learning loop close to the content: understand the moment, save the word, review it later.

Any video source

Watch online videos, open YouTube, or add offline files in formats such as MKV. The player is designed to feel closer to VLC than a narrow course app.

Tap subtitles, save words

Translate unknown words from subtitles, add them to flashcards, and keep watching without breaking the flow.

Translate text from the video frame

Pause the video, detect visible text, and translate it into your native language when subtitles are not enough.

Remember with spaced repetition

Review saved words with flashcards today. More training modes are planned, including sentence building, matching, and letter-based exercises.

A player first, a learning system around it

Use real videos as your input, then choose how much help you want from subtitles, translation, and review.
